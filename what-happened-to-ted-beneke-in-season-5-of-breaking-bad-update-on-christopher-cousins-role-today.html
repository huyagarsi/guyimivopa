What Happened To Ted Beneke In Season 5 Of Breaking Bad? Update On Christopher Cousins Role Today

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Actor Christopher’s cousin Ted Beneke served as president and owner of the company Beneke Fabricators in Breaking Bad, which he inherited from his dad.

Beneke was the lover and Skyler White’s previous boss. He is a divorced dad of twin daughters. In his later years, he took over the family firm and developed strong relationships with his employees.

He turned to big fraud as Beneke Fabricators’ finances started to decrease in order to keep the business alive by falsifying the company’s financial records.

What Happened To Ted Beneke In Season 5 Of Breaking Bad?

In the drama “Breaking Bad” (season 5), Saul Goodman visits the A1A Car Wash to inform Skyler White that Ted is having a problem.

Ted attempted to enlist Skyler’s assistance in falsifying the books once she found out about the tax fraud he was conducting. But in Breaking Bad season 4, the IRS discovered the business’s tax fraud.

Beneke might have gone bankrupt if found guilty and would have faced jail time. He was compelled to pay more than $600,000 in unpaid taxes and fines in order to escape a severe penalty.

Skyler gave him the cash he needed to do it, but Ted declined to utilize it to solve the issue.

Afterward, Skyler ordered two bodyguards to coerce Ted into writing a check to the IRS with the aid of dubious attorney Saul Goodman (Bob Odenkirk).

He was then frightened and made an attempt to flee before stumbling over a rug and breaking his neck severely.

Although it was thought that Ted had passed away, Skyler visited him in the hospital during season 5, and he made a commitment to keep all of her and her family’s secrets private.

Where Is Actor Christopher Cousins Ted Beneke Today?

Saul explains that Ted Beneke has actually awakened from his coma after Skyler, who is unaware of his earlier accident, initially believes he has passed away.

After waking from the coma, he was in really poor condition. It was widely believed, according to his Cousins, that Ted harbored a phobia of the Whites for the rest of his life.

The actor speculated that Ted had quadriplegia as a result of this incident, leaving him bedridden and alone.

After Breaking Bad, he most likely lost his business and most of his other things, even if he did resolve his fraud with the IRS.

One of the unresolved mysteries in Breaking Bad and its follow-up movie, El Camino, was Ted’s status.

Ted might have felt safe speaking with investigators once Walt’s illegal acts became public knowledge.

Why Is Ted Beneke In The Hospital?

Ted Beneke, who is in a state of fear, makes an attempt to flee but stumbles on a rug and collides with furniture, likely sustaining a neck injury.

As a result, he was hospitalized with a severe neck injury. and his time on Breaking Bad came to an end.

Beneke’s terrible neck injury left him in very ill health when Skyler sees him at the hospital. He assures her that he would keep all that has occurred to him private.
