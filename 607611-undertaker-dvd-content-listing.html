Complete Content Listing Revealed for Upcoming Undertaker WWE DVD Box Set

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

WrestlingDVDNetwork.com has revealed the complete content listing for the upcoming WWE DVD box set on The Undertaker.

The set, titled “The Undertaker: The Streak 21-1 (R.I.P. Edition)”, which comes in a coffin box, will be released on November 17th:

Celebrating The Streak

WrestleMania VII • March 24, 1991

The Undertaker vs. Jake “The Snake” Roberts

No Disqualification Match for the WWE Championship

WrestleMania 13 • March 23, 1997

Brother vs. Brother Grudge Match

WrestleMania X-Seven • April 1, 2001
