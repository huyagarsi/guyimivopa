Was Missing Kyle Clinkscales Ever Found? Obituary Dead Body In The Car?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Kyle Clinkscales went to school in 1976 but never returned without a trace. Here’s what we know about the case in this article.

Kyle Clinkscales was a college student who went missing in 1976. After 45 years, police have found Clinkscales’ car in Cusseta, Alabama.

His white Ford Pinto was spotted by a local in a creek in Cusseta and they informed the authorities. Officials from Troup County, Georgia and Chambers County, and Alabama announced that the car belonged to Kyle Clinkscales.

Was Missing Kyle Clinkscales Ever Found? Obituary

Kyle Clinkscales went missing for 45 years but neither police nor his family ever found any answers. The 22-year-old went to school but never returned without any possible answers.

Clinkscales was a college student at Auburn University and was in LeGrange before he went missing.

Kyle Clinkscales Car Found. Was His Dead Body In The Car?

Kyle CLinkscales’ car has been found missing since 1976. After 45 years, police finally found his Ford Pinto in a creek in Cusseta, Alabama.

They found his wallet with his ID and credit cards- there were also bones, but we will have to wait until the authorities confirm the bones found belong to Clinkscales.

The discovery of his car might not be a huge achievement after all these years, but the authorities did try their best to find anything related to the case. They drained lakes, looked everywhere for the young man but all were in vain.

Unfortunately, Clinkscales’ parents could not witness the discovery as his mother passed away in January this year. His father died in 2007. We have no information regarding his living relatives for now.

More information is yet to come regarding this case. But the recent finding has opened up new hope in this case.
