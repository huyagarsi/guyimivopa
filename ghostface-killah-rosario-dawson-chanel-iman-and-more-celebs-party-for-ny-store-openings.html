Ghostface Killah, Rosario Dawson, Chanel Iman and more celebs party for NY store openings

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

When it comes to style, we are all inspired by what we see; whether it be a well-dressed celebrity, a blow-your-mind catwalk presentation or even a fashionable passerby.

As fashion editors, we’re moved by all of the above, and then some. We’re exposed to under-the-radar labels; we get a first-hand look at collections months before they hit stores; we attend VIP events; we’re tapped into brands with chic-yet-cheap offerings and we shop — a lot.

To share our knowledge, FEMAIL brings you Style Swoon, a weekly series of the latest, greatest and on the verge. We hope this Friday series will serve as a buying guide and point of inspiration for all.

This week’s edition: star-studded store openings.

On Friday, October 15, streetwear brand Blue&Cream opened a brand new Upper East Side location

To kick-off the evening, DJ Samantha Ronson and Jus Ske spun live before passing the mic to Ghostface Killah of Wu-tang Clan for a special performance

Blue&Cream

Location: 1196 Madison Avenue, New York NY 

On Friday, October 15, streetwear brand Blue&Cream opened a brand new Upper East Side location. 

The brand new 1,500 square foot location focuses on the shopping”experience” with every detail from smell to lighting, exclusive products, one-on-one appointments and curated DJ mixes. 

‘After 18 years of finding our niche as the place to see and be seen in the Hamptons and Bowery, we are excited to take over Madison Avenue,’ said Blue&Cream Founder Jeff Goldstein.

‘This location will sort of act like a Private Client Studio focusing on the Experience for our loyalists.’

He added: ‘Coming out of isolation and the pandemic, we are seeking the in-store retail experience more than ever. 

‘It’s not just about buying clothes, it’s about providing an environment for our clients to discover — meeting new people, getting style advice from our Specialists, whatever it may be.’

Catering to the Upper East Side residence, the new locale offers a curated assortment of vintage, ready-to-wear and designer for men, women and children

Catering to the Upper East Side residence, the new locale offers a curated assortment of vintage, ready-to-wear and designer for men, women and children.  

Shoppers can find one-of-a-kind pieces from Chanel, NIKE, & Chrome Hearts while also browsing Blue&Cream’s in-house lines like The Tile Club alongside mainstays like Cotton Citizen, RtA, Madeworn, Nanushka, and The Range. 

Brands that Blue&Cream originally put on the map in the Hamptons will also be available to browse, including A.L.C., LoveShackFancy, Golden Goose, Aviator Nation, and more.

Drawing on contemporary art influences, the architecture and design of the new Uptown store is modern with nods to Blue&Cream’s hip-hop and skate culture roots. 

The interior showcases Richard Hambeldon’s original oil-on-canvas works “purple Jumpman” alongside limited-edition pieces from Supreme and KAWS in an uptown parlor styled, pastel-hued white oak private salon. 

To kick-off the evening, DJ Samantha Ronson and Jus Ske spun live before passing the mic to Ghostface Killah of Wu-tang Clan for a special performance.

The night was also a reunion of the original UES “it girls” including Charlotte Ronson Arden Wohl and Dani Stahl. 

Other guests included Catfish’s Nev Schulman, Mischa Barton, Samantha Blake Cohen, Jaime Gleicher and more. 

Guests enjoyed Neapolitan pizza from a truck parked outside while sipping Hampton Water Rosé and Kendall Jenner’s 818 tequila toasting not only the new store but the 18 year anniversary of Blue&Cream’s original opening.

Lafayette 148

Location: 59 Greene Street, New York NY 

On its 25th anniversary, Lafayette 148 New York held a chic soiree in their brand new flagship store in Soho.

The new store is located at 59 Greene Street and was designed by famed architect William Sofield.

New York notables including Rosario Dawson, actress Tiffany Boone, photographer Sophie Elgort and model Lindsay Vrckovnik were dressed to the nines in fashions from the brand.

TWENTY Montréal

Location:  242 Lafayette Street, New York, NY

Luxury active-and knitwear label TWENTY Montréal opened of their first-ever brick and mortar store.

The charming boutique located in SoHo, offers the full TWENTY assortment of casual-yet-refined wardrobe staples for men and women, alongside athleisure and sophisticated activewear.

The aesthetic of the store stays true to the upscale-casual vibe of the brand with a modern, clean and inviting design featuring hardwood floors and white walls and fixtures.
