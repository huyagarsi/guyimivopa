All Star Eight-Man Tag Team Match Set For AEW Worlds End

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 5, 2024

Another match has been made official for AEW Worlds End.

AEW President Tony Khan took to social media on Thursday to announce that eight competitors from the recent Continental Classic will take part in an “All Star” eight-man tag team match. In that bout, Bryan Danielson, Claudio Castagnoli, Mark Briscoe, and Daniel Garcia will battle Jay White, RUSH, Brody King, and Jay Lethal.

These eight competitors all came up short in total points in the Continental Classic as Jon Moxley won the Gold League and Eddie Kingston won the Blue League.

Below is the updated lineup for this Saturday’s pay-per-view in Long Island:

  • : MJF (c) vs. Samoa Joe
  • : Toni Storm (c) vs. Riho
  •  : Christian Cage (c) vs. Adam Copeland
  •  : Jon Moxley vs. Eddie Kingston
  • : Julia Hart (c) vs. Abadon
  • Miro vs. Andrade El Idolo
  • Swerve Strickland vs. Keith Lee
  • Chris Jericho, Sammy Guevara, Darby Allin & Sting vs. Ricky Starks, Big Bill, Powerhouse Hobbs & Kyle Fletcher
  • Bryan Danielson, Claudio Castagnoli, Mark Briscoe & Daniel Garcia vs. Jay White, RUSH, Brody King & Jay Lethal
  • : HOOK (c) vs. Wheeler Yuta (Zero Hour)
  • 20-Man Battle Royale for a shot at the TNT Championship (Zero Hour)

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of the pay-per-view as it airs this Saturday.
