FAQs About Nia Vardalos

Who is Nia Vardalos?

Nia Vardalos is a Canadian actress, screenwriter, and director, best known for her work on the hit film My Big Fat Greek Wedding.

What is My Big Fat Greek Wedding and how did it become popular?

My Big Fat Greek Wedding is a romantic comedy that she wrote and starred in. The film was a massive success, earning her an Academy Award nomination for Best Original Screenplay. Its success was largely boosted by word-of-mouth praise.

Has she won any awards for her work?

Yes, she has received multiple nominations for her work, including an Academy Award nomination for Best Original Screenplay and a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actress in a Motion Picture Comedy or Musical for My Big Fat Greek Wedding.

What other movies has she worked on?

Beyond My Big Fat Greek Wedding and its sequels, she has worked on several other projects including I Hate Valentine’s Day, Connie and Carla, and My Life in Ruins. She also took a serious role in Poisoned Love: The Stacey Castor Story.

What personal challenges has she faced?

She has openly discussed her struggles with infertility. After several attempts with IVF and surrogacy, she and her then-husband Ian Gomez adopted a daughter from the foster care system in 2008.

Has she written any books?

Yes, she wrote Instant Mom, a book where she shares her journey through adoption. She donates all proceeds from the book to adoption groups.

What is her background?

She is of Greek descent, born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. Her cultural background heavily influenced My Big Fat Greek Wedding.

What recent projects has she been involved in?

In recent years, she reprised her role in My Big Fat Greek Wedding 3, where she also served as director and screenwriter. She has also ventured into voice acting and podcasting.

Initially, she faced significant challenges in Hollywood, receiving rejections based on her appearance and ethnicity. These experiences led her to write her own screenplay, which eventually became My Big Fat Greek Wedding.

She and Ian Gomez divorced in 2018 after almost 25 years of marriage. They have one daughter together.
