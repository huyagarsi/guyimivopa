Tom Griswold Biography

Tom Griswold is a renowned American Radio Talk Show Host who was born and brought up in Cleveland, Ohio. Currently, he works at WFBQ radio station in Indianapolis, Indiana as a Co-Host of ‘The Bob & Tom Show’. He works alongside Chick McGee, Kristi Lee, and Josh Arnold.

The Bob & Tom Show which is a comedy-based early morning program is among the highest-rated in American radio and has been nationally syndicated since 1995. He worked on radio in WETO in DeLand, Florida, and WMBN and WJML in Petoskey, Michigan.

Tom met Bob Kevoian in Petoskey, Michigan. After Michigan Griswold would go to WFBQ Radio in Indianapolis in March 1983. He regularly hosts or participates in Bob and Tom Radio: The Comedy Tour shows. On November 5, 2015, Griswold, alongside co-host Bob Kevoian, was accepted into the National Radio Hall of Fame.

    Tom Griswold The Bob & Tom Show

    The Bob & Tom Show is a syndicated US radio program launched by Bob Kevoian and Griswold at radio station WFBQ in Indianapolis, Indiana, on March 7, 1983. The show has been syndicated nationally since January 6, 1995.

    Originally syndicated by Premiere Networks, the show moved to Cumulus Media Networks (now Westwood One) at the beginning of 2014. It enjoys extensive popularity and has frequently been awarded recognition by the National Association of Broadcasters as an exemplar in American radio.

    Tom Griswold Injury

    In mid-July 2009, Griswold was hospitalized for nearly a week due to a broken right arm (humerus bone) and a shoulder injury he suffered after falling from a motorcycle. The injury required surgery that included numerous screws and a plate in his arm. Griswold came back to the air on July 30, 2009.
