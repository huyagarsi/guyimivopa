FAQs about Sofie Gråbøl

Who is Sofie Gråbøl?

Sofie Gråbøl is a renowned Danish actress, famous for her role as Detective Sarah Lund in the TV series The Killing (Danish: Forbrydelsen).

What are some notable roles played by Sofie Gråbøl?

Besides The Killing, Gråbøl has appeared in Taxa, Nikolaj og Julie, and the British series Fortitude.

Has she won any awards?

Yes, she has received multiple accolades including 5 Robert Awards, 2 Bodil Awards, a BAFTA Award, and was nominated for an International Emmy Award in 2008.

Is she married?

She was previously married to Jacob Thuesen, but they divorced in 2006.

Does she have children?

Yes, she has two children, a son named Bror and a daughter named Gudrun.

What challenges has Sofie Gråbøl faced in her personal life?

Gråbøl battled breast cancer in 2012, underwent surgery and chemotherapy, and is now in remission.
