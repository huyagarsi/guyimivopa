Tyrell Terry

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Tyrell Terry is an American professional basketball player for the reputed team ‘Dallas Mavericks’ of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He plays the position of a point guard.

Tyrell Terry is a sensational basketball player who started his career as a college basketball student for the Stanford Cardinal. He has been an NBA player since December 1st, 2020, as the Dallas Mavericks picked and signed a second-round draft pick with him to a four-year contract with the most guaranteed money ($4.6 million).

Tyrell Terry’s Age, Height, Salary

As of 2021, Tyrell Terry is 20 years old. He was born on 2000, 28th September.

Tyrell Terry’s height is 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 m) tall.
