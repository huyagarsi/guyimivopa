All Time Low Release Music Video For &quot;Life Of The Party&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 5, 2024

All Time Low have released a music video for their brand new song, ‘Life Of The Party’. It’s the third song to join the previously released ‘Dirty Laundry’ and ‘Last Young Renegade’ from their upcoming album, Last Young Renegade.

Check out the video for ‘Life Of The Party’ below!

Last Young Renegade is set to be released on 2nd June through Fueled By Ramen and can be pre-ordered here.
