Michael McDonald Biography

Michael McDonald is one of the most popular and richest Poker Player who was born on September 11, 1989 in Waterloo, Waterloo, Canada. Mike McDonald (born September 11, 1989) is a Canadian poker player. McDonald is the youngest person to win an event on the European Poker Tour (EPT) and the youngest person to win an event on the Epic Poker League (EPL).

McDonald finished in 2nd place at the 2014 Pokerstars Caribbean Adventure main event, earning just over $1,000,000 and narrowly missing out on becoming the first player to have two EPT titles.

As of March 2018, his total live tournament winnings exceed $13,200,000.

In September 2011, McDonald won the $20,000 buy-in 8-max No Limit Hold’em tournament at the Epic Poker League earning over $780,000.
