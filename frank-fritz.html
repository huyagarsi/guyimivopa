Frank Fritz: What Happened to the American Pickers Star?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Frank Fritz is a familiar name to the fans of the History Channel’s reality show American Pickers, where he co-hosted with Mike Wolfe for over a decade.

The show follows the duo as they travel across the country, looking for hidden treasures and rare collectables in people’s garages, barns, and junkyards.

But what happened to Frank Fritz, and why did he leave the show?

Here’s everything you need to know about his life, career, and health.

Is Frank Fritz still alive?

The first question that many fans have is whether Frank Fritz is still alive or not.

The answer is yes, he is alive and recovering from a serious health scare.

In July 2022, he suffered a stroke at his home in Iowa and was found unresponsive by a friend.

He was rushed to the hospital and later transferred to a rehab facility for treatment.

His former co-star Mike Wolfe posted about his condition on Instagram, asking fans to pray for his friend.

According to The Sun, Frank has been making progress in his recovery, but he still faces some challenges.

A close friend of Frank told the outlet that he is not debilitated by the stroke, but it has slowed him down and changed his lifestyle.

He also said that Frank has a positive attitude and a great sense of humour, and that he is enjoying being out of the limelight.

Did Frank Fritz have a funeral?

No, Frank Fritz did not have a funeral, as he is not dead.

However, some fans might have been confused by the news of another Frank Fritz’s death in 2020.

This Frank Fritz was an 85-year-old veteran from Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania, who passed away on August 21, 2020.

He had no relation to the American Pickers star, but some online sources mistakenly used his obituary and photo to report on the TV personality’s health.

Who is Frank Fritz?

Frank Fritz is an American reality TV star, antique collector, and antique restorer who was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa.

He has been interested in collecting since childhood, starting with rocks and beer cans.

He later developed a passion for vintage motorcycles, cars, toys, and other memorabilia.

Frank worked as a fire inspector and a motorcycle safety instructor before quitting his job to pursue his hobby full-time.

He opened his own antique store, Frank Fritz Finds, in Savanna, Illinois, where he sells his acquisitions.

Frank also runs a website and a Facebook page where he showcases his collection and interacts with his fans.

He became famous for co-hosting American Pickers with Mike Wolfe in 2010.

The show has been one of the most popular programs on the History Channel, with over 5 million viewers per episode.

What is Frank Fritz doing in 2023?

Frank Fritz has not appeared on American Pickers since March 2020.

He announced that he was taking a break from the show to focus on his health and personal issues.

Frank revealed that he had been diagnosed with Crohn’s disease, a chronic inflammatory bowel condition that causes severe pain and weight loss.

He also admitted that he had struggled with alcohol addiction and had gone to rehab in 2017.

In July 2021, Mike Wolfe confirmed that Frank had left the show permanently and that they were no longer in contact.

He thanked him for his contribution to the show and wished him well.

Since then, Frank has been living a low-key life in Iowa, away from the media spotlight.

He has not given any interviews or updates on his condition or plans for the future.

How is Frank Fritz’s condition today?

As of October 2023, Frank Fritz’s condition is stable but not fully recovered.

He is still undergoing physical therapy and medication for his stroke and Crohn’s disease.

He has lost over 65 pounds since his health crisis and has adopted a healthier diet and lifestyle.

Frank has also quit drinking alcohol and smoking cigarettes.

He spends his time with his family, friends, and dog.

What is Frank Fritz’s net worth?

Frank Fritz has an estimated net worth of $4 million as of 2023.

He has earned this amount from his successful career as an antique collector and TV star.

Frank also made money from his store, website, book deals, and endorsements.

He owns several properties in Iowa and Illinois, as well as a collection of vintage vehicles and items.

Who is Frank Fritz’s wife?

Frank Fritz didn’t have a wife.

He has been very private about his personal life and has rarely spoken about his romantic relationships.

He was once rumoured to be dating Diane Ashmore, a woman who appeared on American Pickers as one of his clients.

However, neither of them confirmed or denied their relationship status.

In 2015, Frank was sued by a woman named Susan Bednar, who claimed that he had caused her emotional distress and breached a contract.

She alleged that Frank had promised to marry her and buy her a house, but then backed out and cut off contact with her.

She also accused him of being unfaithful and abusive. He denies the allegations and the case was settled out of court.

Did Frank Fritz have a stroke?

Yes, Frank Fritz had a stroke in July 2022, which was the main reason for his departure from American Pickers.

A stroke occurs when the blood supply to a part of the brain is interrupted, causing brain cells to die.

This can result in various symptoms, such as:

  • weakness
  • numbness
  • vision problems
  • speech difficulties
  • confusion
  •  memory loss

It can be caused by various factors, such as:

  • high blood pressure
  • diabetes
  • smoking
  • obesity
  • stress

Frank’s stroke was reportedly mild and did not cause any permanent damage to his brain.

However, it did affect his mobility and speech, and he had to undergo intensive rehabilitation to regain his functions.

He also had to take medication to prevent another stroke and to manage his Crohn’s disease.

Conclusion

Frank Fritz is a former American Pickers star who left the show in 2020 due to his health issues.

He suffered a stroke in 2022 and has been recovering ever since.

He is also living with Crohn’s disease, a chronic condition that affects his digestive system.

Frank has not returned to the show or the public eye and has been focusing on his well-being and happiness.

He is still an avid antique collector and enthusiast, but he is no longer in touch with his former co-host Mike Wolfe.

He is one of the most beloved personalities on TV, and we hope he gets better soon.
