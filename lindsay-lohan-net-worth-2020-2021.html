Early Life

Lindsay Lohan was born on 2nd July 1986 in New York City. Her father was a stock trader, and her mother was a singer cum dancer. She started homeschooling after 11th grade as she underwent a turbulent childhood experience as her parents divorced and reunited a couple of times. Inspired by her mother, Lindsay had a natural inclination to singing. Lindsay also started as a child model and did modeling for Ford Models, Calvin Klein, and several such popular brands for commercials.

Personal Life

Her personal life has always been controversial. She had been part of a love triangle in 2001, and she had dated multiple TV personalities like Wilmer Valderrama, Harry Morton, Calum Best, Riley Giles, and even Russian real estate agency owner Egor Tarabasov. Furthermore, she dated another girl DJ Samantha Ronson.

Age, Height, and Weight

Career

Lindsay debuted with Disney comedy TV film The Parent Trap, which was very successful. She was cast in other Disney TV movies Life-Size and Got a Clue. In 2003, she was the lead actress in the comedy movie Freaky Friday. But it was her role in the movie Mean Girl in 2004 that made her popular in Hollywood.

To utilize her popularity, she achieved her long-term dream with the music album Speak and A Little More Personal in 2004 and 2005. The following two years were good for Lindsay as she was part of several movies. But her career suffered a lot as she got addicted to drugs and had various minor accidents leading to the loss of her wealth.

From 2007 to this year, she had literally disappeared from movies. She was arrested for driving under alcohol effects. She was also arrested on a different occasion for possessing drugs like cocaine. Her health also wakened badly, and she had to be admitted to the hospital for kidney and liver infection.

She also underwent surgery. She spent several months throughout these years in rehab. She was mentally and economically broken. She appeared in some TV series and movies for guest appearances. In June 2017, she started her lifestyle site named Premium.

Lindsay Lohan received Young Artist Award for her role in The Parent Trap in 1999. In 2004, she won MTV Movie Award for Best Breakthrough Performance and Teen Choice Award in 2004. For her role in Mean Girls, she received MTV Movie Awards for Best Female Performance and Best On-Screen Duo. She is also the recipient of Kids’ Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actress in 2006.
