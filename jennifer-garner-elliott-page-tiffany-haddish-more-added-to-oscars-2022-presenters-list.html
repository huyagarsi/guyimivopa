Jennifer Garner, Elliott Page, Tiffany Haddish &amp; More Added to Oscars 2022 Presenters List

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 14, 2024

There are some new additions to the 2022 Oscars presenters list!

On Monday (March 21), producers Will Packer and Shayla Cowan announced that Elliot Page, Jennifer Garner, Tiffany Haddish, Bill Murray, Encanto‘s Stephanie Beatriz, DJ Khaled, H.E.R., Tony Hawk, Kelly Slater and Shaun White will be presenters at the 94th annual Academy Awards.

Click inside to read more…

Previously announced presenters include Lady Gaga, Lily James, Zoe Kravitz, Chris Rock and more. This year’s show is set to be hosted by Amy Schumer, Wanda Sykes and Regina Hall.

Last week it was revealed that Jason Momoa and Josh Brolin will be presenting the awards for the eight categories that have been cut from the live broadcast. The categories, which will be awarded in an hour-long ceremony before the telecast, include original score, makeup and hairstyling, documentary short, film editing, production design, animated short, live action short and sound.

The 2022 Oscars will be held Sunday at Hollywood’s Dolby Theatre and televised live on ABC at 8 p.m. ET. You can see the full list of nominees here.
