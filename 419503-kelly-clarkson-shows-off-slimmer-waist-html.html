Kelly Clarkson Shows Off Slimmer Waist in Belted Jeans &amp; White Top after Noticeable Weight Loss

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 31, 2024

Country music sensation and beloved talk show host Kelly Clarkson recently took to social media to share an exciting new venture, announcing her partnership with Bob's Backstage Popcorn. While the news of the partnership was undoubtedly noteworthy, Clarkson's striking appearance captured her fans' attention.

Known for her powerful voice and dynamic presence, Kelly Clarkson has always been a figure of admiration. In her latest appearance, she donned a simple T-shirt paired with black jeans, cinched at the waist with a belt. This seemingly casual outfit, however, highlighted her notable weight loss, drawing immediate praise from her fans.

One admirer commented, "Girl, you look 20 years younger." Another echoed this sentiment, enthusiastically remarking, "Can we just stop and take a sec to say how great you look!!! You are just glowing in this new phase of life!!!" Adding to the chorus of compliments, another fan observed, "Wow, Kelly, you really lost weight. You look good."

Addressing her weight loss journey, Clarkson shared a candid moment with her audience at a show in Las Vegas. She humorously noted that she had been wearing tighter clothing post her weight loss, quipping to a fan that bending over might result in a wardrobe malfunction due to the snug fit of her outfit.

The "Since U Been Gone" singer has been open about her challenges with fashion, particularly in finding jeans that accommodate her body shape. She candidly expressed her dislike for jean shopping, citing the difficulty in finding a pair that comfortably fits both her larger posterior and smaller waist.
