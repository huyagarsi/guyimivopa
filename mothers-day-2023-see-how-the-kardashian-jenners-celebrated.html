Mother's Day 2023: See How the Kardashian-Jenners Celebrated

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 27, 2024

A very Kardashian Mother’s Day is underway! The reality TV family kicked off the Sunday, May 14, holiday with lots of fanfare.

“I feel so blessed with the most thoughtful daughter,” Kourtney Kardashian gushed via Instagram Story early in the day revealing 10-year-old Penelope decked out her mom’s bedroom with rose petals and flameless candles.

The Poosh blogger, 43, also received a thoughtful Mother’s Day tribute from her husband of one year, Travis Barker. “Not a better mama bear than you @kourtneykardash,” the Blink-182 drummer, 47, shared via his Instagram Stories. “[The] best BMX rider. … Looking stunning at the same time. I love you.”

The Lemme founder — who shares Penelope and sons Mason 13, and Reign, 8, with ex Scott Disick — then took her kids to her mother Kris Jenner’s house for a special breakfast. Her sisters Kim Kardashian, Khloé Kardashian, Kylie Jenner and Kendall Jenner also joined the fun, as well as their respective children. (Kendall, 27, is currently the only Kardashian-Jenner sibling without kids.)

“Armenian beeshee in pajamas,” Kourtney captioned a second Instagram Story pic, showing off the traditional pancakes that the brood munched on, along with fresh fruit and pastries.

The festive food was plated on lemon-themed dishes, which matched the yellow roses that were displayed in the center of the table.

After they ate, the 67-year-old matriarch surprised all of her kids with special gifts, which had been wrapped in yellow paper and tied with white ribbons. “Happy Mother’s Day,” Kris penned on the cards. “I love you, Mommy.”

The Safely founder also further paid tribute to her special job as a mother on Sunday. “Happy Mother’s Day to all of my babies and my mom MJ,” Kris wrote via Instagram hours before the bash alongside a carousel of throwback family photos. “You are my whole heart and soul and I thank God every single day that He blessed me with you!!!!”

She added: “I am such a lucky girl!! I love you so much my beautiful family ❤️😍🙏🏼🥰 Wishing all the moms out there a happy Mother’s Day!”

Kylie, for her part, made sure to get in plenty of snuggles with her two children: Stormi, 5, and Aire, 15 months, during the big day.

“Happy Mother’s Day,” the Life of Kylie alum — who shares her little ones with ex Travis Scott — wrote via Instagram alongside a selfie with Stormi and Aire in their pajamas.
