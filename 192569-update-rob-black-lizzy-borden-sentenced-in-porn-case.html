Update: Rob Black &amp; Lizzy Borden Sentenced In Porn Case

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 24, 2024

The Pittsburgh Post-Gazette recently reported the sentencing of Rob Black & Lizzy Borden (Rob Zicari and his wife, Janet Romano) to one year and one day. In addition to running Extreme Associates and creting and distributing pornography, Black was also the owner of XPW, a California based wrestling company.

In more positive but somewhat related news:

Former XPW star Luke Hawx (Altar Boy Luke) Luke Hawx has been quite busy since coming back from a 6 month torn
