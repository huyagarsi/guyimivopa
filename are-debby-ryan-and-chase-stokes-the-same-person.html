The Bizarre Reason Fans Think Debby Ryan And Outer Banks Star Chase Stokes Are The Same Person

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes are great actors in their own rights. Ryan got her start as a child actor on Disney Channel, while Stokes waited tables until he got his big break on Netflix’s Outer Banks in 2020. Ryan was also a Netflix star, leading the streaming network’s Insatiable. Ryan married Twenty-One Pilot’s Josh Dun in 2019, and Stokes has had a celebrity relationship as well.

Their work as actors and high-profile dating lives are not the only things about Ryan and Stokes that have fans talking about them. In recent years, fans have circulated the rumor that the two actors are secretly the same person. The root of this is their physical features, specifically their face shape and eyebrows. They do look very similar, so let’s get into the bizarre rumor that Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes are the same person.

8 Who Is Debby Ryan?

Debby Ryan is an actress who got her start on the Disney Channel. Fans will remember her adorable character of Bailey Pickett on The Suite Life Of Zach And Cody On Deck. She then went on to star as the main character of Disney’s show Jessie.

Since her exit from Disney Channel, Ryan has kept herself busy with acting jobs. She made a name for herself with Netflix by starring in their show Insatiable. She also recently starred alongside Jorge Lendeborg Jr. and Lucy Fry in the film Night Teeth.

7 Who Is Debby Ryan’s Husband Josh Dun?

Debby Ryan is famously married to musician Josh Dun. Dun is a part of the hit band Twenty-One Pilots with Tyler Joseph. Dun is highly successful in the music industry, and fans of the couple love seeing Ryan support her husband. She even travels around the world with Dun while he is on tour.

The two dated on and off for years before finally tying the knot in 2019. They chose to get married on New Year’s Day after their year-long engagement. The couple look so happy in their wedding photos.

6 Who Is Chase Stokes?

Chase Stokes is relatively new to the public eye. Before fans new him as his beloved character on Netflix, Stokes was actually waiting tables and living in his car. He stepped onto the scene after being cast as the lead in Netflix’s Outer Banks. He plays John B., a rebellious character searching for gold and his family’s redemption.

When the show first hit the streaming service, fans fell in love with Stokes and his character. Stokes has focused on his work on Outer Banks, but fans can see him in a guest role in Amazon Prime’s Tell Me Your Secrets.

5 Did Chase Stokes Date Outer Banks’ Madelyn Cline?

Fans were over the moon when they learned that Chase Stokes and Madelyn Cline took their on-screen relationship in Outer Banks into the real world. In June 2020, soon after the show first aired, the two announced their relationship on Instagram.

Unfortunately, their relationship was not able to last the hardships of actor life. The distance of shooting apart, specifically while Cline was filming Knives Out2, caused the two to split after a little over a year together.

4 Fans Think Debbie Ryan And Chase Stokes Look Alike

The rumor that Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes are the same person all started on TikTok. One user pointed out on the social media platform that Ryan and Stokes look really similar—so similar that they could be the same person!

Fans pointed out that not only do the two celebrities have the same face shape, but they also have the same eyebrows. In some photos, the two even smile the same. This led fans to come up with the theory that Ryan and Stokes could secretly be the same person.

3 Are Debby Ryan And Chase Stokes Related?

Fans thought Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes looked so similar that the two celebrities had to at least be related. People on social media thought the two were maybe distant cousins. Fans searched for a familial connection between Ryan and Stokes.

However, fans found nothing online linking the two celebrities. Despite what many fans assumed, Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes are not related. They just happen to look really similar.

2 Chase Stokes Served Debby Ryan

Though Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes are not related, they do have a small connection. While fans are dying to see them play brother and sister in a production, the two actors have never worked together. They have met on at least one occasion though.

Ryan revealed to E! News that she met Stokes years ago, even though she did not realize it until much later. She took her family to a Brazilian steakhouse in Universal City. “Years later Chase Stokes told me that he was our server and that we interacted.”

1 Debby Ryan And Chase Stokes Address The Rumor

The theory that Debby Ryan and Chase Stokes are secretly the same person got so big online that Ryan actually addressed the rumor. Ryan squashed the hysterical rumor that she and Stokes are not the same person. She found the theory quite amusing and thought it spoke to how creative people can be online.

Stokes also responded to the rumor. His response was “the internet is a weird place.” He also added “thank you crazy people for putting me and Debby in contact. Debby is a homie.”
