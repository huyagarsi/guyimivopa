Dennis Rodman Back Tattoo Meaning Explained On Twitter

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Dennis Rodman’s brightly colored and constantly changing hairstyles, as well as his tattoos, set him apart from the rest of the world’s most famous basketball players.

Dennis Rodman is well-known for his tenacious defense and rebounding skills.

He began his career as a small forward before advancing to the power forward position. Dennis was named to the NBA All-Defensive First Team seven times and twice received the NBA Defensive Player Award.

Dennis Rodman Back Tattoo Meaning Explained

Dennis Rodman’s chest is covered with two colossal enraged bulls, one on each side. The two angry bulls appear poised to lock horns on his chest.

The Bulls exemplify the former power forward’s style of play: aggressive, belligerent, and fearless.

The most famous tattoo is on his back, and it depicts a woman attempting to eat her private body parts.

On his tummy, he has a large tribal tattoo.

He also has a tattoo of an oversized sun with a shark bursting through the middle, a red dice, another tribal design, and ink of a motorcycle.

How Many Rebounds Did He Have In His Career?

Dennis Rodman is one of only two players in NBA history to have accumulated 5,000. Dennis had 11,954 rebounds and 6,683 points in his college and professional careers.

Bill Russell, who had 21,620 rebounds and 14,522 points, is the other player with similar numbers.

Rodman is regarded as one of the all-time great rebounders in NBA history.

Dennis had a career-high 34 rebounds in a game in March 1992. Despite calf difficulties at the beginning of the 1995–1996 season, he collected 20 or more rebounds 11 times.

Moreover, on January 16, 1996, he scored 10 points and added 21 rebounds and ten assists.

Dennis Rodman Wife: Does He Have Children?

In September 1992, he married his first wife, Annie Bakes. Their daughter, Alexis Caitlyn, was born in 1988. Infidelity and accusations of abuse damaged their marriage. After 82 days of marriage, the couple divorced.

Dennis married model Carmen Electra at the Little Chapel of the Flowers on November 14, 1998, but sought an annulment nine days later, alleging he was of “unsound mind” when they married.

They reunited, but in April 1999, Electra filed for divorce.

Dennis married Michelle Moyer in 1999, and they have a son, Dennis Jr., and a daughter, Trinity. On his 42nd birthday, they married in 2003.

Michelle Rodman filed for divorce in 2004, despite the couple’s attempts to reconcile for several years. In 2012, the marriage was declared null and void.

In 2019, his son D. J. began playing college basketball for Washington State. His daughter, Trinity, plays professional soccer with the Washington Spirit.

Net Worth Of Dennis Rodman In 2022

Dennis Rodman was selected in the second round of the 1986 NBA draft by the Detroit Pistons.

His first-year remuneration was only $75,000. That figure had risen to $2.3 million by the time he left Michigan in 1993.
