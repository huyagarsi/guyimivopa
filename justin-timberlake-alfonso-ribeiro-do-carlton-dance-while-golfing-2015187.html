Justin Timberlake, Alfonso Ribeiro Do Carlton Dance While Golfing

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

Alfonso Ribeiro will never say bye, bye, bye to The Carlton Dance — and that’s fine by Us. The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air actor pulled out the old-school move with none other than Justin Timberlake during the American Century Celebrity Golf Tournament in Lake Tahoe, Nevada, on Friday, July 17.

Ribeiro, 43, gave the crowd a nostalgic thrill after a bad shot. New dad Timberlake cheered him up by also joining in on the fun. The guys laughed it off and shook hands before heading to the next hole.

“Great day in Tahoe Edgewood with @jtimberlake and Johnathan Thomas of American Century,” Ribeiro tweeted on July 15. On Friday, the “Mirrors” singer, 34, retweeted a hilarious photo of his pals holding him up by his legs. He quipped: “BEST. GOLF. HOLE. EVER.”

Ribeiro previously kicked up his heels and did The Carlton Dance during Season 19 of Dancing With the Stars. (He and partner Witney Carson took home first place!) “I sometimes get asked to do The Carlton when I’m in bathrooms,” he told Us Weekly in October 2014.

He added: “It’s never okay when you’re at a urinal and someone taps you on the shoulder and says, ‘Dude, you’ve gotta do the Carlton for me!’ Really? Really?!”
